The 2016 presidential race is on. Two main candidates, Donald Trump and Hillary Clinton, compete to be the next president for the United States.

Donald Trump

On June 14,1946, Donald Trump was born in New York City, New York. He is currently 70 years old. Trump later attended the University of Pennsylvania for college. Trump is an American businessman who is an author, politician, and the Republican nominee for the 2016 Presidential Race. He is now the chairman of the Trump Hotels and Casino Resorts. Trump also founded the Trump Organization. He is married to Melania Trump and has five children. His children are Ivanka, Tiffany, Barron, Eric, and Donald Jr.
